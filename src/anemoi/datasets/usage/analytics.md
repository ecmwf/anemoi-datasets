## Anemoi dataset analytics

Knowing how often datasets are used helps us manage our large catalogue more effectively: it allows us to retire datasets that are no longer needed, and to ensure that popular ones are pre-installed and readily available on key sites.

You can help by opting in to share usage information with the Anemoi team. This is the information that would be sent if analytics were enabled:

You can opt in or opt out at any time using the `anemoi-datasets analytics` command.
